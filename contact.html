Research Partnership

EdTerm is looking for
the right partners.

We are at the research stage — actively seeking university partners, academics, journalists, and organizations who believe that lived human experience is the most undervalued knowledge resource on earth. If that belief resonates, we want to hear from you.

Who We're Looking For

The people who
build this with us.

University Research Partners

Academic institutions interested in co-developing the Wisdom Journalism curriculum, conducting research on knowledge preservation and transfer, or stewarding regional Wisdom Databases as part of their community mission.

Journalism Educators & Programs

Faculty and programs open to restructuring elements of their curriculum around Wisdom Journalism — training students to elicit, structure, and preserve lived human experience as a new form of public knowledge.

Researchers & Academics

Scholars working in narrative psychology, knowledge management, oral history, AI ethics, education, or human development with interest in the theoretical and empirical foundations of the Human Wisdom Database.

Organizational Partners

Companies, nonprofits, or government bodies looking to preserve institutional knowledge, support workforce mentorship at scale, or pilot EdTerm as a tool for identity development and experiential learning.

Mentors & Wisdom Contributors

Experienced professionals from any field or background willing to contribute their lived experience to the Human Wisdom Database — helping future learners navigate decisions you've already faced.
